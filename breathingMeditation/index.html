Just Breathe

About

I built this small application as a way to help me focus during this unsettling time of the COVID-19 pandemic. I was feeling overwhelmed with everything going on in the world, and having trouble concentrating on react and vue and algorithms and accessibility, performance, build tools, package managers, testing frameworks, and the gazillion other things necessary to learn in order to become a front-end developer in 2020. So I decided to build something to help people intentionally focus on their breath. The idea is that when you are focused on your breath, you are slowing down and becoming grounded in the present moment.

Inhale as the flower expands. Exhale as it contracts. Just... breathe.
